Welcome To The CAC Module

*Introduction:* The Laser Cutting Module at our Fab Lab provides users with the tools and knowledge to create precise and intricate designs on a variety of materials using laser cutting technology. This documentation serves as a guide for using the laser cutting equipment safely and effectively. *Table of Contents:* 1. *Safety Guidelines* - Proper attire and personal protective equipment - Material compatibility and ventilation - Emergency procedures 2. *Getting Started* - Equipment overview: laser cutter components and controls - Material preparation: selecting, sizing, and securing materials 3. *Software and Design* - Design software: introduction to vector design programs (e.g., Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW) - File setup: creating vector files, setting cut and engrave parameters - Importing and exporting design files 4. *Machine Operation* - Powering on/off and system initialization - Loading and unloading materials - Setting cutting parameters: speed, power, frequency - Running test cuts and adjustments 5. *Laser Cutting Techniques* - Vector cutting: creating precise outlines and shapes - Raster engraving: adding textures and gradients - Combined operations: cutting and engraving in a single design 6. *Maintenance and Troubleshooting* - Regular maintenance: cleaning lenses, mirrors, and exhaust system - Diagnosing and addressing common issues - Contact information for technical support 7. *Advanced Features* - Rotary attachment: engraving on cylindrical objects - Camera alignment: using built-in cameras for precise positioning - Layered designs: utilizing multiple passes for intricate effects
